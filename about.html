Our Sacred Story

Founded in the spirit of craftsmanship and fellowship

The Genesis of Last Sip

At The Last Sip, every cup is a canvas and every gathering a symphony. Inspired by the Renaissance, we celebrate beauty, curiosity, and the art of living well. Here, coffee is more than a drink — it is a moment of rebirth, crafted to awaken both heart and mind. Step inside, and let time slow. Welcome to a place where past and present meet in timeless harmony.

Here, every pastry is a small masterpiece, every sip of coffee a quiet verse. We believe in gathering as the thinkers and dreamers once did — not just to eat and drink, but to share stories, laughter, and inspiration. The Renaissance was an age that cherished light, beauty, and human connection. In that same spirit, our café is a sanctuary where you can pause, reflect, and let simple moments feel eternal.

Our Fellowship

M

Hok Lim Seyha

Founder & Head Roaster

Inspired by da Vinci’s Last Supper, Seyha founded The Last Sip to blend art, craft, and community into every cup. As head roaster, he treats coffee like a canvas — balancing precision with passion — so each sip feels timeless, a small taste of the Renaissance brought into the present.

I

Heng Eavheang

Co-Founder & Culinary Artisan

With a spirit rooted in Renaissance creativity, Heng Eavheang brings artistry to the table at The Last Sip. As culinary artisan, every pastry and dish is crafted as a masterpiece — balancing flavor, texture, and beauty — to complement each cup of coffee. Together with the team, Heng ensures every guest experiences not just a meal, but a taste of timeless elegance.

S

Lun Kimchea

Master of Ceremonies

At The Last Sip, every gathering is guided by the charm and presence of Lun Kimchea. As Master of Ceremonies, he carries the Renaissance spirit of connection — welcoming guests, weaving stories, and turning each visit into a shared experience. With his touch, the café becomes not only a place to sip, but a stage for conversation, laughter, and timeless moments.

Our Sacred Philosophy

"In every carefully crafted cup, we taste not just coffee, but the essence of art, discovery, and the human spirit that has inspired generations."

At The Last Sip, we hold sacred the belief that coffee is more than a drink—it is an experience, a celebration of creativity, and a bridge between minds and hearts. Inspired by the Renaissance, we honor the craft in every roast, the beauty in every pastry, and the connection in every shared moment. Here, each sip invites reflection, inspires conversation, and reminds us that even the simplest pleasures can carry the grandeur of timeless elegance.
